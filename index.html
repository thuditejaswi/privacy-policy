Last updated: February 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect user information when using our WhatsApp-based business application and related services.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our services, we may collect the following information:

  • WhatsApp phone numbers
  • Messages sent and received through WhatsApp
  • Message timestamps and delivery status

2. How We Use the Information

The collected information is used only for legitimate business purposes, including:

  • Sending automated WhatsApp messages

3. User Consent

By interacting with our WhatsApp number, you explicitly consent to receiving messages from us and agree to this Privacy Policy.

Users can opt out of messages at any time by stopping communication on WhatsApp.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or trade user data to third parties.

Data is shared only with Meta Platforms, Inc. (WhatsApp) as required to provide WhatsApp Business messaging services.

5. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user data from unauthorized access, misuse, loss, or disclosure.

Data is stored only for as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

7. Compliance with WhatsApp Policies

Our application complies with the WhatsApp Business Platform policies and Meta Developer policies. We send messages only in accordance with WhatsApp’s rules and user consent requirements.
